Refactory is a pioneer training academy in Uganda, offering alternative industry-led skilling for the global technology industry. It is focused on equipping learners with skills to enable them to venture into a Tech career of choice.

Our Story
Refactory is a Company Limited by Guarantee that was formed by four partners; Laboremus Uganda Ltd, Fontes Foundation,
Youth With A Mission UK, and Clarke International University. It has been supported by funding from the Norwegian Agency for International Development (NORAD) and other donors.
